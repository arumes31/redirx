1. Introduction

Welcome to Redrx ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our URL shortening service.

3. Information We Collect

A. Data when you visit a link (Redirects)

When someone clicks a link shortened via Redrx, we collect the following data to provide statistics to the link creator and for security purposes:

  • IP Address: Used for rate-limiting, security, and determining approximate geographic location.
  • User Agent: Browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Referrer: The website you came from.
  • Timestamp: The date and time of the click.

B. Data when you create an account

If you register for an account, we collect:

  • Username & Email Address: To manage your account and authentication.
  • Hashed Password: We never store your actual password, only a secure hash.
  • API Key: Generated for your programmatic access.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: To provide the service you signed up for (Account management).
  • Legitimate Interest: To ensure the security of our platform (Rate limiting), to prevent abuse (Phishing protection), and to provide anonymized analytics to link creators.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party processors to run our service:

  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for security and performance. Cloudflare may process your IP address to protect against DDoS attacks.
  • MaxMind: We use MaxMind GeoLite2 databases to convert your IP address into an approximate country (e.g., "Germany"). Your IP is processed locally on our server against their database.

6. Data Retention

  • Click Logs: Detailed click data (IPs) is stored to provide stats but may be anonymized or deleted after a set period.
  • Account Data: Stored as long as your account remains active.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You may also object to processing or request data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided above.

8. Cookies

We use a single "Session Cookie" which is strictly necessary for the login functionality of the dashboard. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.
